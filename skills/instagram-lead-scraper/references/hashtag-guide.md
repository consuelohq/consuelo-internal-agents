## Hashtag Strategy

**Best Combinations:**
1. `#finalexpense` + `#agentshelpingagents` - Highly qualified
2. `#lifeinsuranceagent` + `#entrepreneur` - Business builders
3. `#insuranceagent` + location tag - Local agents

**Avoid:**
- Oversaturated tags (>10M posts) - too broad
- Irrelevant tags - wastes time
- Spam hashtags - low quality

## Post Quality Indicators

**Good Signs:**
- Recent post (last 7-30 days)
- Consistent posting (1-3x/week)
- Engagement (10+ comments)
- Professional bio
- Clear insurance focus

**Red Flags:**
- No posts in 6+ months
- Generic content
- No engagement
- Unclear business focus
